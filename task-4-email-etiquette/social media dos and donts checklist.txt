Social Media Do’s and Don’ts (For College Students)

Do’s:

1. Think before posting and ensure your content is respectful and appropriate.
2. Maintain privacy settings to protect your personal information.
3. Use social media for learning by following educational and informative content.
4. Be respectful while communicating with others in comments and messages.
5. Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation.

Don’ts:

1. Do not share personal details such as phone number, address, or passwords publicly.
2. Do not engage in cyberbullying or post offensive or harmful content.
3. Do not spend excessive time on social media and avoid distractions.
4. Do not post inappropriate or unprofessional content that may affect your future.
5. Do not trust unknown people or click on suspicious links, as they may be unsafe.
